01 / Thesis

We were operators before we were investors. We still are.

In 2003 our principals bought Grand Canyon University. Nine-hundred students. Weeks from foreclosure. We rebuilt it into the largest private Christian university in the country.

Lopes Capital is what happened after. We deploy capital the way we ran the school — pencil-down, owner-mind, no PowerPoint between us and the asset. Most LPs we work with care less about the deck than the call after the deal closes. We agree.
